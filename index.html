THESIS PROGRESS

Introduction

This site is about Sage Won's thesis project.

Inspired by machine-age aesthetics, project explores the machine and making as well as exploring the idea of typography. Including generative arts into the design process, within the scope of this project, it engaged in a series of design experiments involving a variety of shapes and grid systems into type forms.



It is to create and express ideas of type by understanding how to interface with machines. Abstract forms were used in order to illustrate the concept behind the value of each alphabets and to create tension within composition.
